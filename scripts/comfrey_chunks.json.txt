Plant Identity & Names Comfrey is a robust perennial herb that stands out in the landscape of temperate farms and gardens. It belongs to the borage family, Boraginaceae, and comes in two closely related forms that growers should distinguish: Symphytum officinale L. – Common Comfrey. Ancient medicinal lore: Comfrey’s use dates back at least two millennia in Europe.
